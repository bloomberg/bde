 bbldcu.txt

@PURPOSE: Provide simple day-count implementations.

@DESCRIPTION: The 'bbldcu' package provides simple day-count implementations;
 for conventions that only need two dates for the 'daysDiff' and 'yearsDiff'
 methods.

/Hierarchical Synopsis
/---------------------
 'bbldcu' package currently has 7 components having one level of dependency.
 The table below shows the hierarchical ordering of the components:
..
  1. bbldcu_actual360
     bbldcu_actual365fixed
     bbldcu_isdaactualactual
     bbldcu_isma30360
     bbldcu_psa30360eom
     bbldcu_sia30360eom
     bbldcu_sia30360neom
..

/Component Synopsis
/------------------
 The following is a brief synopsis of the components in 'bbldcu':
..
  bbldcu_actual360             - Actual/360 convention
  bbldcu_actual365fixed        - Actual/365 (fixed) convention
  bbldcu_isdaactualactual      - ISDA Actual/Actual convention
  bbldcu_isma30360             - ISMA 30/360 convention
  bbldcu_psa30360eom           - PSA 30/360 end-of-month convention
  bbldcu_sia30360eom           - SIA 30/360 end-of-month convention
  bbldcu_sia30360neom          - SIA 30/360 no-end-of-month convention
..
