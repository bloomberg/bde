Purpose:
Support connecting through a network of SOCKS5 proxies.
MNEMONIC: Basic Transport Environment Socks5 (btls5):
Description:
The btls5 package supports connecting through a network of SOCKS5 proxies. The top-level interface is provided by btls5_NetworkConnector.
