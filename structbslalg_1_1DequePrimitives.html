Detailed Description

template<class VALUE_TYPE, int BLOCK_LENGTH>
struct bslalg::DequePrimitives< VALUE_TYPE, BLOCK_LENGTH >

This struct provides a namespace for a suite of utility functions that operate on deques parameterized by the VALUE_TYPE and BLOCK_LENGTH. Depending on the traits of VALUE_TYPE, the default and copy constructors, destructor, assignment operators, etc. may not be invoked, and instead the operation can be optimized using a no-op, bit-wise move or copy.
