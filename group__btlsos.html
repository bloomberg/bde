Purpose:
Provide concrete TCP/IPv4 channels derived from btlsc protocols.
MNEMONIC: Basic Transport Environment SOcket-based Streams (btlsos):
Description:
The btlsos package provides concrete imlpementations of btlsc channels using TCP/IPv4 sockets. Timed and untimed, and blocking and non-blocking versions of generic channels, acceptors, and connectors are provided.
