Purpose:
Provide application-level support services.
MNEMONIC: Basic Application Environment (bal):
Description:
The bal ("Basic Applications Environment") package group contains packages that provide application-level support services in varied areas such as encoders/decoders, timezone support, command-line handling, metrics, and logging.
