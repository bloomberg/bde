Purpose:
Provide versioning information for btl library components.
MNEMONIC: Basic Transport Environment Source Control Management (btlscm):
Description:
The btlscm package is identical in design and purpose to the bdescm package, except that btlscm is incorporated into each btl component to provide uniform versioning information for that package group.
Hierarchical Synopsis:
